Engineering Skills Discussion:

The engineering discipline is established through a combination of academic study and the application of principles in a work environment. It is necessary to demonstrate an ability to creatively apply the scientific principles to design 1.  A candidate should have evidence of these qualifications.

I have formal academic training. My engineering degree is a B.S. in Computer Engineering2 from Lehigh University, Bethlehem, Pa. The Computer Engineering major was fairly new at the time that I was admitted into Lehigh's College of Engineering. This major was founded upon the classical Electrical Engineering major but with a concentration in Digital Circuit Architectures and Design Algorithms.

While employed at General Electric's Space Systems Division in King of Prussia, Pa3  I studied for my M.S. in Engineering Science4 at Penn State's Ogontz Campus, which is now called the Abington campus5. I successfully completed 15 credit hours toward that degree with a 3.8 GPA.
